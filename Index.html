Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon species in Nintendo and Game Freak's Pokémon franchise. Designed by Atsuko Nishida, Bulbasaur debuted in Pocket Monsters: Red and Green as a starter Pokémon.

  • Type: Grass, Poison
  • Weaknesses: Psychic, Fire, Flying, Ice
  • Ability (hidden): Chlorophyll
  • Ability: Overgrow
